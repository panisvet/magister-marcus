- **Levels 3–4:** Ray's Practical Arithmetic, Robinson's Progressive Arithmetic
- **Levels 5–6:** Wentworth's New School Algebra (1898), Ray's Higher Arithmetic

**36 weeks | ~180 lessons | Pre-K through early Grade 2**

## Level 1 Week-by-Week Outline

### UNIT A: Numbers 0–10 (Weeks 1–8) — Gentle Introduction
*8–12 problems per lesson, no speed drills, heavy picture work*

| Week | Topic | Key Skills | Enrichment |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Numbers 1–5: Recognition & Counting | Counting objects 1–5, writing numerals, one-to-one correspondence | Latin: unus, duo, tres |
| 2 | Numbers 1–5: More/Fewer/Same | Comparing groups, matching, ordering | Roman numerals I–V |
| 3 | Number 0 and Numbers 0–5 Review | Zero as "none," counting backward 5–0 | Tally marks 1–5 |
| 4 | Numbers 6–10: Recognition & Counting | Counting 6–10, writing numerals | Latin: sex, septem, octo |
| 5 | Numbers 6–10: More/Fewer/Same | Comparing groups 0–10, ordering | Roman numerals VI–X |
| 6 | Numbers 0–10: Mixed Review & Ordering | Number line 0–10, before/after/between | Mayan dot-bar 1–5 |
| 7 | Introduction to Addition: Joining Groups | Concept of "putting together," +1 and +2 with pictures | Story problems with pictures |
| 8 | Introduction to Subtraction: Taking Away | Concept of "taking away," −1 and −2 with pictures | Finger counting strategies |

### UNIT B: Addition & Subtraction Facts 0–10 (Weeks 9–16)
*12–16 problems per lesson, speed drills begin Week 13 (oral only, 25 facts)*

| Week | Topic | Key Skills |
|---|---|---|
| 9 | Addition facts: sums to 6 | +0, +1, +2, +3 with manipulatives |
| 10 | Addition facts: sums to 8 | +4, +5; doubles (2+2, 3+3, 4+4) |
| 11 | Addition facts: sums to 10 | +6 through +9, doubles to 5+5 |
| 12 | Subtraction facts: from 6 and under | −0, −1, −2, −3; number bonds |
| 13 | Subtraction facts: from 8 and under | −4, −5; fact families begin; first speed drills |
| 14 | Subtraction facts: from 10 | −6 through −9; fact families 0–10 |
| 15 | Mixed addition & subtraction 0–10 | Review all facts; word problems |
| 16 | Assessment Week: Facts 0–10 | Knowledge check, enrichment: Roman numerals I–X review |

### UNIT C: Numbers 11–20 & Teen Facts (Weeks 17–22)
*16–20 problems, written speed drills 25 problems begin*

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 17 | Numbers 11–15: reading, writing, place value (1 ten + units) |
| 18 | Numbers 16–20: reading, writing, ordering |
| 19 | Addition with teen sums (e.g., 9+4, 8+6) |
| 20 | Subtraction from teens (e.g., 13−5, 17−8) |
| 21 | Mixed facts 0–20; word problems |
| 22 | Assessment Week: Facts 0–20 |

### UNIT D: Place Value & Numbers to 100 (Weeks 23–28)
*20 practice problems, 25-problem speed drills*

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 23 | Tens and ones: grouping into tens |
| 24 | Numbers 20–50: reading, writing, expanded form |
| 25 | Numbers 50–100: skip counting by 2s, 5s, 10s |
| 26 | Comparing numbers to 100 (<, >, =) |
| 27 | Adding and subtracting tens (30+20, 70−40) |
| 28 | Assessment Week: Place value to 100 |

### UNIT E: Addition & Subtraction to 100 Without Regrouping (Weeks 29–32)

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 29 | Adding 2-digit + 1-digit (no regrouping) |
| 30 | Adding 2-digit + 2-digit (no regrouping) |
| 31 | Subtracting 1-digit from 2-digit (no regrouping) |
| 32 | Subtracting 2-digit from 2-digit (no regrouping) |

### UNIT F: Ordinals, Patterns & Review (Weeks 33–36)

| Week | Topic |
|---|---|
| 33 | Ordinal numbers 1st–10th; patterns (AB, AAB, ABC) |
| 34 | Word problems: addition and subtraction mixed |
| 35 | Measurement: longer/shorter, heavier/lighter; time: hour |
| 36 | End-of-Level Review & Placement Assessment for Level 2 |
