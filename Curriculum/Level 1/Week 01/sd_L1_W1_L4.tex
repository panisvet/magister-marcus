\begin{instructbox}
\noindent\textbf{\color{sd-blue}Instruction:}\\[4pt]
When one group has more objects than another, we say it has \textbf{more}.
When it has fewer, we say \textbf{fewer}.
When they match exactly, we say \textbf{same}.
\end{instructbox}
