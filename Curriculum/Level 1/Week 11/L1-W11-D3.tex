\begin{lessonbox}
\textbf{You now know all the addition facts with sums to 10!}\\[4pt]
Here are the strategies that help:\\[2pt]
\begin{tabular}{@{}ll@{}}
$+0$, $+1$: & same or one more \\
$+2$, $+3$: & count on from bigger \\
$+4$, $+5$: & count on (5-hand helps) \\
Doubles: & $1{+}1$ to $5{+}5$ \\
Near-doubles: & double $\pm 1$ \\
$+9$: & $+10$ then back 1 \\
Ten-pairs: & $1{+}9$, $2{+}8$, $3{+}7$, $4{+}6$, $5{+}5$ \\
\end{tabular}
\end{lessonbox}

\noindent\textbf{19.} There are \textbf{6} birds on a fence. Four more land. How many birds on the fence?

\medskip\noindent\hspace{2em} $\numbox + \numbox = \numbox$ birds

\bigskip

\noindent\textbf{20.} A jar holds \textbf{10} buttons. Mary has \textbf{3} in her pocket. How many more does she need to fill the jar?

\medskip\noindent\hspace{2em} $3 +$ \numbox $= 10$\quad She needs \numbox\ more.
