\begin{lessonbox}
\textbf{What is Addition?}\\[4pt]
Addition means \textbf{putting groups together} to find out how many \textit{in all}.\\[6pt]
We use the sign \textbf{+} to mean ``and'' or ``plus.''\\
We use the sign \textbf{=} to mean ``equals'' or ``is the same as.''\\[8pt]
\begin{center}
\begin{tikzpicture}[scale=1]
  % left group: 3 dots
  \foreach \i in {1,2,3}{\filldraw[scholablue](\i*0.5-0.2,0)circle(0.18cm);}
  \node at (2.1, 0) {\Huge\textcolor{scholared}{$+$}};
  % right group: 2 dots
  \foreach \i in {1,2}{\filldraw[scholagreen](\i*0.5+2.6,0)circle(0.18cm);}
  \node at (4.1, 0) {\Huge\textcolor{scholared}{$=$}};
  % all group: 5 dots
  \foreach \i in {1,...,5}{\filldraw[scholagold](\i*0.5+4.4,0)circle(0.18cm);}
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{center}
\begin{center}
  \Large $3 \;+\; 2 \;=\; 5$
\end{center}
\medskip
\noindent We say: \textit{``Three plus two equals five.''}
\end{lessonbox}
