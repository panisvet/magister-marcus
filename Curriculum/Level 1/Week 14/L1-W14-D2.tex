\begin{instructbox}
\textbf{Subtracting 8 and 9} — use the Ten-Partners shortcut:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{$-9$:} \quad $10-9=1$, $9-9=0$ \quad Think: ``9 + \textit{what} = 10?'' \quad $9+1=10$, so $10-9=1$
  \item \textbf{$-8$:} \quad $10-8=2$, $9-8=1$, $8-8=0$ \quad Think: ``8 + \textit{what} = 10?'' \quad $8+2=10$, so $10-8=2$
\end{itemize}
\end{instructbox}
