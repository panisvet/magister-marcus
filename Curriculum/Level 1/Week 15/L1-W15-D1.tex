\begin{instructbox}
This week we practise \textbf{all} addition and subtraction facts from 0 to 10 together.\\[4pt]
\textbf{Read the sign carefully!} The $+$ sign means \emph{join}. The $-$ sign means \emph{take away.}\\
Use any strategy: count on, count back, doubles, ten-partners, or fact families.
\end{instructbox}

\bigskip
\begin{checkbox}
\textbf{\color{sd-green}Addition and Subtraction are Partners!}\\[3pt]
Every addition fact has a subtraction partner, and every subtraction fact has an addition partner.\\[3pt]
If $7+3=10$, then $10-3=7$ and $10-7=3$.\\[3pt]
\textit{Knowing one fact gives you two (or three!) facts for free.}
\end{checkbox}
