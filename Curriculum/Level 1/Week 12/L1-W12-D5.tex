\medskip

\noindent\textbf{18.} Tom had \textbf{5} toy cars. He gave \textbf{2} to a friend. How many cars does he have now?

\medskip\noindent\hspace{2em} $5 - 2 =$ \numbox\ cars

\bigskip

\noindent\textbf{19.} Six birds sat on a branch. Three flew away. How many remain?

\medskip\noindent\hspace{2em} $6 - 3 =$ \numbox\ birds

\bigskip

\noindent\textbf{20.} A basket had some apples. Four were taken out. Now there is \textbf{2}. How many were in the basket?

\medskip\noindent\hspace{2em} \numbox\ $- 4 = 2$\quad There were \numbox\ apples.
