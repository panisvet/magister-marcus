# *The New World Made Ready* — Unit 2
## Notebooking Pages — Form IIA
### The American Founding, 1763–1789

---

**How to Use These Pages**

Each page pairs a historical illustration with writing lines and a narration prompt.

- **Study the illustration:** Observe detail — expressions, clothing, objects, light. What moment is depicted? What is about to happen?
- **Narrate in writing:** Write a full paragraph in response to the prompt. Aim for 5–8 complete sentences. Use specific names, places, and dates from what you have read and heard.
- **Copywork:** One passage per page is marked for dictation practice.

*Form IIA: Written paragraph narration. Your teacher may also assign these as dictation passages.*
