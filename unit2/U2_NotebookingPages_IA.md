### The American Founding, 1763–1789

---

**How to Use These Pages**

Each page in this section pairs a historical illustration with writing lines and a narration prompt.

- **Illustrate:** Look closely at the picture. Notice faces, objects, and what is happening.
- **Narrate:** Your teacher will read the prompt aloud. Tell back what you see and what you know.
- **Write:** Copy one sentence into the lines below the picture — your best handwriting.

*Form IA: Write 1–3 complete sentences. You may narrate aloud instead of writing.*
