Year 6 · Term 2 — Teacher Answer Key

Week 13 · Clauses &amp; the Simple Sentence
1) subject Greeks, verb built. 2) Yes — it is an independent clause (simple sentence).
Week 14 · Compound Sentences; Comma &amp; Semicolon
1) “…soldiers; Athens…”. 2) “…soldiers, and Athens trained thinkers.”
Week 15 · Dependent Clauses; Complex Sentences
1) “Although he was young.” 2) No — it depends on the main clause to finish the thought.
Week 16 · Adjective (Relative) Clauses
[that Judah drove] describes chariot.
Week 17 · Adverb &amp; Noun Clauses
a) ADV (when…)   b) NOUN (what the oracle meant = object of knew).
Week 18 · Compound-Complex; Sort All Four
a) S   b) CD   c) CX   d) CC.
Week 19 · Verb Tense &amp; Consistency
“…lifted the oar and pulled with all his strength.”
Week 20 · Subject–Verb–Complement
1) S=senate, V=honored, C=general (DO). 2) S=general, V=was, C=hero (predicate noun).
Week 21 · Objects; Predicate Nouns &amp; Adjectives
a) temple = DO   b) powerful = PA   c) teacher = PN.
Week 22 · Participles &amp; Participial Phrases
“Chained to the oar” describes Judah.
Week 23 · Gerunds &amp; Infinitives
a) Waiting = gerund (subject)   b) to rule = infinitive.
Week 24 · Term Review — Classify Every Verb
was = linking, past; built/traded/prospered = action, past.
