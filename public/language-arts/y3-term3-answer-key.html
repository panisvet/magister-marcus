Year 3 · Term 3 — Teacher Answer Key

Keep this separate from the student booklet. Score Conventions on the child’s own pen only.

Week 25 · Compound Sentences (and / but / or)
e.g. “The wolves howled, and the night was cold.”
Week 26 · The Comma with the Conjunction
…roared, but Mowgli did not run.
Week 27 · Complex Sentences (because / when / if)
any sensible reason, e.g. “…because winter was near.”
Week 28 · Relative Clauses (who / which / that)
e.g. “the narrow gate that led to life.”
Week 29 · Compound-Complex Sentences
whole: “the river rose” + “the animals fled”; when-part: “When the rains came.”
Week 30 · Sort All Four Sentence Types
a) S   b) CD   c) CX   d) CC.
Week 31 · Simile
any fitting comparison, e.g. “as quiet as a mouse.”
Week 32 · Metaphor
e.g. “The night sky is a dark blanket sewn with stars.”
Week 33 · Personification
e.g. “The old gate groaned as it swung.”
Week 34 · Studied Copywork
Talk about how the sentence is built — where it joins, where it pauses.
Week 35 · Imitating a Sentence Shape
Praise a sentence that follows the model's rhythm, not its exact words.
Week 36 · Capstone — Imitation &amp; Recitation
Ready for the imitation term when he can narrate fully, mark the parts of a sentence, and imitate a shape.
