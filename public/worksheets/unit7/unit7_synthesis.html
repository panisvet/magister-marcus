Form IIA · Written Narration
Write five paragraphs — one for each body region. Use at least 14 vocabulary words.
Paragraph 1 — The scaffold: cells to organism hierarchy, the four tissue types, the skeleton as a system of levers (give one example of class and advantage)
Paragraph 2 — Heart and blood: the two loops of circulation, what William Harvey proved in 1628, how Nightingale's sanitation reforms applied circulatory physiology
Paragraph 3 — Breath and food: gas exchange in the alveoli, the digestive sequence from mouth to absorption, how these two systems depend on the circulatory system
Paragraph 4 — Nerves and senses: voluntary vs. involuntary, the reflex arc, transduction (how each sense converts a physical stimulus into a nerve signal)
Paragraph 5 — Florence Nightingale: the three stories (the calling, Scutari mortality, the statistician), and how her work required knowledge of at least three body systems
