✦ A Note on Deep Time
"In the beginning God created the heaven and the earth." — Genesis 1:1 (KJV)
Scientists say the solar system formed about 4.6 billion years ago from a cloud of gas and dust. This idea is part of the deep-time framework — an interpretation of the evidence built on assumptions about how the universe works. Our faith teaches that God made the heavens and the earth. We can learn what scientists say, look carefully at what is in front of us, and hold what the Church teaches — all at the same time. How do we know that?
