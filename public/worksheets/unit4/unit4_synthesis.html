Form IIA · Written Narration
Write four paragraphs. Use at least 12 vocabulary words.
Paragraph 1 — Earth in space: rotation vs. revolution, why axial tilt causes seasons (not distance from sun), the shadow stick as evidence
Paragraph 2 — The moon: what causes phases (not shadow), the difference between a phase and an eclipse, your moon tracking journal observations
Paragraph 3 — The solar system: the toilet paper scale model and what it proved, terrestrial vs. gas giant planets, why the Galilean moons mattered
Paragraph 4 — Stars and the universe: spectroscopy as a way of knowing, what a light-year means, the difference between observation and interpretation in deep-space claims
