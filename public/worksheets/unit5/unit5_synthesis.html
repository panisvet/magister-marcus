Form IIA · Written Narration
Write four paragraphs. Use at least 12 vocabulary words.
Paragraph 1 — Minerals and rocks: the identification tests, the three rock families and how each forms, what the rock cycle reveals about the earth's constant motion
Paragraph 2 — Fossils: conditions required for fossilization (rapid burial), index fossils and the circularity of their dating, what the Cambrian Explosion shows about the history of animal life
Paragraph 3 — Hutton and Lyell: what Hutton observed at Siccar Point, what Lyell built from it, the difference between an observation and the interpretation placed on it
Paragraph 4 — Framework: what uniformitarianism claims, what catastrophism claims, what the Missoula Floods tell us about which is always correct, and what the Orthodox Church teaches about the earth's history
