# Notebooking Page (NBP) Generation Prompts
## Wonders of the Created World — Unit Synthesis Pages

All pages: parchment cream (#f5f0e4) background, dark brown ink (#1e140a), Georgia serif font, aged botanical illustration aesthetic matching the Charlotte Mason classical curriculum style. Portrait orientation, letter size (8.5×11). Each page has a decorative vine or botanical border motif, a header with unit number and title, and two clearly delineated sections: Form IA (younger, oral/drawing) and Form IIA (older, written).

## General Design Notes for All Pages

- **Paper:** Letter size portrait, 300dpi minimum
- **Margins:** 0.6in top/bottom, 0.65in left/right
- **Typography:** Georgia or IM Fell English for body; Cinzel or small-caps for headers
- **Colors:** Parchment cream #f5f0e4, ink #1e140a, moss green #2d5a27 (Unit 1/2/3), slate blue #1a2a4a (Unit 4/7), warm brown #4a3010 (Unit 5/6), gold accent #8b6914
- **Lines:** Rule lines at 17pt height with 1pt stroke in #c8b48a
- **Decorative borders:** Thin double rule at top and bottom of page; unit-specific botanical or scientific motif in corners
- **Footer:** "Schola Domestica · Wonders of the Created World · Unit [N] Synthesis" in 8pt small caps
