Form IIA · Written Narration
Write four paragraphs — one for each topic. Use at least 10 vocabulary words.
Paragraph 1 — The seed and germination: what is inside a bean seed, what happens at germination, why Buckley calls a seed a pause not a beginning
Paragraph 2 — Flowers and pollination: what a flower is actually for, how pollination works, what the ovule becomes
Paragraph 3 — Seed dispersal: the five dispersal strategies, why dispersal matters, which is most ingenious and why
Paragraph 4 — Photosynthesis and deep time: the chemical equation in plain English, what tree rings reveal, where the carbon in a tree trunk actually comes from
